Manuscript Review Process
We follow a rigorous and transparent peer review process to ensure the highest standards of academic quality, originality, and integrity. Our double-blind peer review system protects reviewer and author anonymity while maintaining editorial oversight.

1
Manuscript Submission
Authors submit manuscripts through our online portal with all required documents. Submissions must include cover letter, abstract, keywords, and comply with our formatting guidelines.

2
Initial Screening (3-5 days)
Our editorial team performs a desk review to verify that the manuscript meets scope, quality, and formatting standards. Manuscripts not meeting basic criteria are desk-rejected with feedback.

3
Double-Blind Peer Review (15-20 days)
Accepted manuscripts are assigned to 2-3 expert peer reviewers in the relevant field. Both reviewers and authors remain anonymous to ensure objective assessment. Reviewers evaluate originality, methodology, significance, and presentation.

4
Editorial Decision (5-7 days)
The Editor-in-Chief reviews all reviewer feedback and makes a final decision: Accept, Minor Revisions, Major Revisions, or Reject. Authors receive detailed feedback from reviewers to guide revisions.

5
Revision & Resubmission (Variable)
For manuscripts requiring revisions, authors address reviewer comments and resubmit with a detailed response letter explaining changes. Revised manuscripts undergo additional review as needed.

6
Publication (Accepted manuscripts)
Accepted manuscripts undergo copyediting and typesetting. Articles are published online immediately with permanent DOI, included in the next quarterly issue, and made available through all indexed databases.
