# Design Guidelines: Scholar India Publishers Website

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach with Academic Focus**: Drawing inspiration from professional academic publishing platforms like JSTOR, Springer, and IEEE Xplore, while incorporating the user's specific blue and gold color requirements. The design prioritizes credibility, readability, and easy navigation for researchers and academics.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Background: Deep professional blue (220 25% 15%) 
- Content boxes/cards: Rich gold yellow (45 85% 65%)
- Text on blue: White/light gray for high contrast
- Text on gold: Dark blue (220 25% 15%) for readability

**Supporting Colors:**
- Light blue accent (220 35% 85%) for subtle highlights
- Dark gold (45 70% 45%) for borders and emphasis
- White for content backgrounds within gold boxes

### B. Typography
**Font Families:**
- Headers: Merriweather (serif, academic feel)
- Body text: Inter (clean, readable sans-serif)
- UI elements: Inter

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 2.5rem, bold
- H2: 2rem, semibold  
- H3: 1.5rem, medium
- Body: 1rem, regular
- Small text: 0.875rem

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Units:** Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, and 16 (p-4, m-8, space-y-12, etc.)
- Consistent 4-unit base for tight spacing
- 8-unit for standard component spacing
- 12-unit for section breaks
- 16-unit for major layout divisions

### D. Component Library

**Core Components:**
- **Header**: Blue background with white logo/text, horizontal navigation
- **Journal Cards**: Gold yellow boxes with rounded corners, containing journal information
- **Content Sections**: White background containers within the blue layout
- **Statistics Cards**: Gold yellow boxes displaying metrics (citations, papers, etc.)
- **Partner Logos**: White background section with certification badges
- **Navigation**: Clean blue background with white text links
- **Forms**: White backgrounds with blue accent borders
- **Buttons**: Primary (gold), Secondary (white with blue text), Outline (white border on blue)

**Layout Patterns:**
- Full-width blue background throughout
- Centered content containers (max-width)
- Grid layouts for journal showcases (3-column on desktop)
- Card-based design for all major content blocks

### E. Content Structure

**Homepage Sections:**
1. Header with navigation and logo
2. Hero section with main title and brief description
3. Three journal showcase cards (gold boxes)
4. About section (white content box)
5. Statistics section (gold metric cards)
6. Partner logos section (white background)

**Individual Journal Pages:**
- Dedicated pages for each journal with detailed aims, scope, and submission info
- Consistent gold box styling for content sections

### F. Visual Hierarchy
- Blue backgrounds create consistent branding
- Gold yellow boxes draw attention to key content (journals, stats)
- White content areas provide reading comfort
- Clear typography hierarchy guides information consumption

## Images
No large hero images required. The design relies on:
- Partner certification logos in the footer area
- Small icons for statistics (citations, papers, etc.)
- Clean, logo-focused header without imagery
- Focus on typography and color-based visual impact

This design approach ensures professional credibility while making the user's specific blue and gold color scheme central to the visual identity.
